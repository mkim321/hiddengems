Sustainability

Hidden Gems is dedicated to innovating women’s vintage while setting a standard for sustainability in the fashion industry. Our goal in fashion is to push the boundaries of contemporary style. We want women to feel confident with their style by encouraging them to break the boundaries of fast fashion trends.

We understand the impacts the fashion industry has on the environment so we make every effort to reduce waste at each step of our obtaining process. We do our part in salvaging second hand and vintage garments that would typically end up in landfills. In order to eliminate textile waste, all of our textile scraps are repurposed and recycled whereas many brands throw their scraps into the garbage.

Every time a second hand garment is purchased, it lowers the demand for new, unsustainable garments to enter the market . The way we spend our money means something, and when we’re thoughtful about our purchases we can set new standards that influence change in the fashion industry and benefit our planet.

We like to focus on customer engagement on all of our platforms. We enjoy giving personalized service to all our shoppers. Transparency with our customers is a priority for us. We want our customers to be in the know about our sustainability initiatives, vintage picking and all about our shipping process.

We also use 100% compostable packaging made out of plant-based materials to reduce any further waste in the shipping process. After planting the mailer in soil it will decompose into organic matter.
