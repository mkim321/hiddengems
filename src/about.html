About us

Our mission as a company is to create a space for sustainable fashion to exist, online and in our local community. We pride ourselves in taking care of our customers and employees and giving them the ability to flourish and explore their personal style.

We are Hidden Gems, an online and brick and mortar thrift shop based in Vancouver, British Columbia. We specialize in collecting and curating one of a kind second hand pieces. Our shop came about when we noticed there was a gap between sustainable fashion and eCommerce. Our passion for creating a unique style and breaking out of fast fashion cycles is what sparked the creation of Hidden Gems.

As children we learned to appreciate hand-me-downs and working with what we had. But as we grew older we saw the rise of materialism and following “trends”. This increasingly became an environmental and social issue around the globe. However within social media apps such as Tik Tok we noticed a rise in people purchasing reworked, vintage and thrifted clothing. This inspired us to bring a second hand shop to eCommerce.
