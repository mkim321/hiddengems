How it works

It’s free and easy to donate with Hidden Gems.

1. Package your clothing items

Free up space in your closet by filling your package with gently used items you no longer wear, need, or love.

2. Send us your stuff

We provide the shipping label to send your package to our warehouse, or you can make your way to our warehouse to donate your things on site.

3. We do the rest!

Your work here is done! We will take your items off your hands and find them a brand new home!
